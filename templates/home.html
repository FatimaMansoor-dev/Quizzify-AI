What type of questions can I create?

+

You can create multiple-choice, true-false, and fill-in-the-blank questions. QuizXpert offers flexibility to accommodate different question formats, making it perfect for a variety of quizzes and assessments.

Can I use content from PDF documents to create quizzes in QuizXpert?

+

Absolutely! QuizXpert can parse PDFs and automatically generate questions from the text. Simply upload your PDF, and our AI will handle the rest.

How can I input my content into QuizXpert for question generation?

+

You can paste text directly, upload a PDF, provide a URL, or even enter a YouTube link. QuizXpert's AI then analyzes and generates relevant questions and answers.

Can I customize the questions generated by QuizXpert?

+

Yes! After generating a quiz, you can easily tweak questions, difficulty level, and more to ensure the final quiz meets your needs.
